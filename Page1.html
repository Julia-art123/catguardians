Welcome to Cat Guardians! Your guide to improving the welfare of cats through care, adoption, and community solutions.

Did you know that? There are over 1 billion cats globally, and nearly half of them are stray, struggling to survive in various environments.
In Canada alone, while there are 8.5 million pet cats, there are still up to 4 million stray cats struggling to survive.
Many of these stray cats, especially kittens, face poor health and disease, with 25% not surviving beyond six months.
Despite this, their high reproductive rates continue to have a significant impact on local ecosystems, even leading to the extinction of certain species.
Unfortunately, even many household cats do not receive adequate care, with regular vet visits and proper health management often being neglected. Many lost cats are never found and eventually join the stray population.

Our website, Cat Guardians, is dedicated to improving this situation. We aim to help pet owners learn how to care for their cats properly, ensuring their health and happiness. At the same time, we provide resources for addressing the stray cat issue, promoting TNR (Trap-Neuter-Return) programs and the importance of adoption. By engaging with our platform, you can learn how to become a responsible cat owner and also play a role in supporting stray cats.

Our Mission

We are dedicated to improving the lives of both domestic and stray cats. By promoting responsible cat care, adoption, and community involvement, we aim to reduce the number of homeless cats and ensure that every feline finds a safe and loving home.
